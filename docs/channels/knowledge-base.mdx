You can use the Knowledge Base to answer most questions about your product or service to the visitors. 

## Setup a Channel

1.	First, create a widget, then edit the widget
  <Image src="channels/create-widget" alt="Bit-Assist" w="1046" h="300"/>
 
2.	Click on add channel to add the support assistant of your choice.
  <Image src="channels/create-click-add-channel" alt="Bit-Assist" w="1046" h="300"/>

3.	Search & Select Knowledge Base from the channels popup
  <Image src="channels/knowledge-base/select-knowledge-base" alt="Bit-Assist" w="1046" h="300"/>

4. **Channel Title:** Set the proper Channel Title. Default it's taken, Custom Form. This Title is descriptive text for visitors.
  <Image src="channels/knowledge-base/title" alt="Bit-Assist" w="1046" h="300"/>

5. **Custom image:** You can use a custom image or gif in the button, which will make the button attractive
  <Image src="channels/knowledge-base/custom-image" alt="Bit-Assist" w="1046" h="300"/>
  <Video src="knowledge-base-custom-image" w="1020" h="350" /> 


6. **Add FAQ:** Here you can set the FAQ title nd here description. You can customize the text in bold,italics,color,insert images, text align,links and etc in the FAQ description. 
  <Image src="channels/knowledge-base/description" alt="Bit-Assist" w="1046" h="300"/>
  <Image src="channels/knowledge-base/add-view" alt="Bit-Assist" w="1046" h="300"/>

7. **Form Theme Color:** You can change the form theme color of your preference.
  <Image src="channels/knowledge-base/theme-color" alt="Bit-Assist" w="1046" h="300"/>

8. **Form Text Color:** You can change the form test color of your preference.
  <Image src="channels/knowledge-base/text-color" alt="Bit-Assist" w="1046" h="300"/>

9. **Hide after office hours:** You can organize your office time by enabling the Hide after office hours option that you have already configured from settings.
  <Image src="channels/business-hours" alt="Bit-Assist" w="1046" h="300"/>

10. **Channel show on:** You can now afford to hide or show channels on mobile and desktops as of your choice with this feature.
  <Image src="channels/hide-and-show-channel" alt="Bit-Assist" w="1046" h="300"/> 

11. **Save:** Now save the channel by clicking the Save button. Then you will see a successful channel-created message.
  <Image src="channels/knowledge-base/save" alt="Bit-Assist" w="1046" h="300"/>

12. **Search**: The visitor can search faq easily if you have ad lot of Knowledge Base.
  <Image src="channels/knowledge-base/front-view" alt="Bit-Assist" w="1046" h="300"/>
  <Image src="channels/knowledge-base/search" alt="Bit-Assist" w="1046" h="300"/>
