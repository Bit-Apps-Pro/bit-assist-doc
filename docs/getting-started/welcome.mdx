Warmly welcome to Bit-Assist. One of the most helpful WordPress plugins - Bit Assist. Connect all your support assistants with a single button and communicate with your customers using different messaging apps. Connect with visitors using Facebook Messenger, WhatsApp, Skype, SMS, Tawk, Telegram, Viber, Slack, etc. Also, notable features are Knowledge Base, Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ), Custom Contact Form, Webhook, etc.
