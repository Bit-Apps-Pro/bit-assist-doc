## Setup a Channel

1.	First, create a widget, then edit the widget
  <Image src="channels/messenger/create-widget" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
 
2.	Click on add channel to add the support assistant of your choice.
  <Image src="channels/messenger/create-click-add-channel" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
 
3.	Search & Select the channel of your choice from the channels popup
  <Image src="channels/messenger/search-and-select" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
 

4.	**Channel Title:** Set the proper Channel Title. Default it's taken, *Messenger*. This Title is descriptive text for visitors.
  <Image src="channels/messenger/channel-title" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
 
5.	Input your ***facebook profile or page user name*** 
  <Image src="channels/messenger/username" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>

6.	With this `On click open channel on` feature of Widget Options, You can open your channel in a new tab, or current tab, or a new window. Default it's taken, New Tab
  <Image src="channels/messenger/open-channel" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
 
7.	With this `Hide after office hours` feature of Widget Options, You can organize your office time by enabling the Hide after office hours option. You have to upgrade to pro to use the feature.
  <Image src="channels/messenger/business-hours" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
 
8.	With this `Channel show on` feature of Widget Options, you can now afford to hide or show channels on mobile and desktops as of your choice.
  <Image src="channels/messenger/hide-and-show-channel" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>

9.	Now save the channel by clicking the `Save` button. Then you will see a successful channel-created message.
  <Image src="channels/messenger/save" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
  <Image src="channels/messenger/successful-message" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
  <Image src="channels/messenger/messenger" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
