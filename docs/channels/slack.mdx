Slack is a popular platform for team communication, and it can be integrated with your website using the Bit-Assist plugin. With this integration, you can streamline your customer support messaging and provide a better experience for your users. Setting up Slack as a channel is a simple process that can be done in just a few steps. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process of integrating Slack with Bit-Assist so that you can start using this powerful tool to enhance your website's messaging capabilities.


## Setup a Channel

1.	First, create a widget, then edit the widget
  <Image src="channels/create-widget" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
 
2.	Click on add channel to add the support assistant of your choice.
  <Image src="channels/create-click-add-channel" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
 
3.	Search & Select the channel of your choice from the channels popup
  <Image src="channels/slack/search-and-select" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
 

4.	**Channel Title:** Set the proper Channel Title. Default it's taken, *Slack*. This Title is descriptive text for visitors.
  <Image src="channels/slack/channel-title" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
 
5.	Input your ***Slack workspace unique-id*** 
  <Image src="channels/slack/channel-username" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
<details>
      <summary>
       <b>How to get your Slack workspace unique-id</b>
      </summary>
      <div>
          <ul>
            <li>First, Login your Slack account</li>
            <li>Click on your workspace name that is in the top left corner.
                  <Image src="channels/slack/click-workspace" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
              </li>
            <li>To edit the details of your Slack workspace, click on the workspace name or logo, which is at the top of the pop-up.
                  <Image src="channels/slack/slack-workspace-edit-details" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
             </li>
            <li>Copy your workspace id in the URL field.
                <Image src="channels/slack/copy-workspace-url" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
             </li>
            <li>Then paste into the Slack workspace the unique-id field of your channels popup.</li>
         </ul>
      </div>
</details>

6.	With this `On click open channel on` feature of Widget Options, You can open your channel in a new tab, or current tab, or a new window. Default it's taken, New Tab
  <Image src="channels/open-channel" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
 
7.	With this `Hide after office hours` feature of Widget Options, You can organize your office time by enabling the Hide after office hours option. You have to upgrade to pro to use the feature.
  <Image src="channels/business-hours" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
 
8.	With this `Channel show on` feature of Widget Options, you can now afford to hide or show channels on mobile and desktops as of your choice.
  <Image src="channels/hide-and-show-channel" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>

9.	Now save the channel by clicking the `Save` button. Then you will see a successful channel-created message.
  <Image src="channels/save" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
  <Image src="channels/slack/successful-message" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
  <Image src="channels/slack/window-show" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
