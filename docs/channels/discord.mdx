Discord is a popular communication platform designed for creating communities, organizing discussions, and connecting with others through voice, video, and text. By using Bit Assist with Discord, you can add a Discord chat button to your website, allowing visitors to engage with you through Discord's messaging.   
Setting up Discord as a channel is a simple process that can be completed in just a few steps. Our guide will take you through these steps so that you can start using this powerful tool to enhance your website's messaging capabilities.

## Setup a Channel

1.	First, create a widget, then edit the widget
  <Image src="channels/create-widget" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
 
2.	Click on add channel to add the support assistant of your choice.
  <Image src="channels/create-click-add-channel" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
 
3.	Search & Select the channel of your choice from the channels popup
  <Image src="channels/discord/search-and-select" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
 

4.	**Channel Title:** Set the proper Channel Title. Default it's taken, *Discord*. This Title is descriptive text for visitors.
  <Image src="channels/discord/channel-title" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
 
5.	Input your ***Discord Invite code*** 
  <Image src="channels/discord/channel-username" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>


<details>
      <summary>
       <b>How to get your Discord Invite code</b>
      </summary>
      <div>
          <ul>
            <li>First, Login your Discord account</li>
            <li>Navigate your discord server</li>
            <li>Click the edit button on the right of the channel.
                  <Image src="channels/discord/click-edit-channels-setting" alt="Discord setting - Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
              </li>
            <li>Navigate to 'Invites' and copy the Invite code if already generated. However, if there is no code generated yet, please click on the 'create one' button to generate the code.
                  <Image src="channels/discord/copy-invite-code" alt="Discord setting - Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
             </li>
            <li>Then paste into the Discord Invite code field of your channels popup.</li>
         </ul>
      </div>
</details>


6.	With this `On click open channel on` feature of Widget Options, You can open your channel in a new tab, or current tab, or a new window. Default it's taken, New Tab
  <Image src="channels/open-channel" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
 
7.	With this `Hide after office hours` feature of Widget Options, You can organize your office time by enabling the Hide after office hours option. You have to upgrade to pro to use the feature.
  <Image src="channels/business-hours" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
 
8.	With this `Channel show on` feature of Widget Options, you can now afford to hide or show channels on mobile and desktops as of your choice.
  <Image src="channels/hide-and-show-channel" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>

9.	Now save the channel by clicking the `Save` button. Then you will see a successful channel-created message.
  <Image src="channels/save" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
  <Image src="channels/discord/successful-message" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
  <Image src="channels/discord/window-show" alt="Bit Assist" w="1046" h="200"/>
